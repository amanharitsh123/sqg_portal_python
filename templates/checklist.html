Software Productization Self-Assessment Checklist

NIC is involved in design and development of large scale e-governance applications. Number of these applications are reusable across Ministries/Departments/State Governments/Organisations and can also be replicated for government departments in other countries. For the applications to be reusable it needs to be easily configurable, customizable and should be able to meet the key requirements related to design, usability, performance, scalability, maintainability, security, data management, audit etc. Similarly, for product to be reusable across countries, it should support globalization/localization features.
